    Join us. We are looking for...

    Ruby on Rails developer

    Who we are

    Want to help build things like the Panama Papers website & DB, an open government open source platform, tools to make it easy to develop and work with public data, visualizations to make it easy to understand public finances and engage the public? Those are some of the things you will be doing at Populate.

    Populate is a design & dev studio that conceptualizes, designs and builds products for civic engagement: tools and platforms that enable citizens and organizations to better understand and interact with the world around us. We create our own projects and provide commercial services around data journalism, news products, open data and civic engagement.

    We value a lot how we get along and how everyone fits in our self-managing, your own schedule, choose your projects environment. We would prefer someone in Madrid, Spain, so we can regularly meet and work together in our office, although all of us work many days from home or from other remote places.

    What we need

    • Developer with deep knowledge of Ruby on Rails framework and Ruby language in general
    • Appreciate code quality and agile development processes, such as TDD, small iterations and git PR workflow
    • Interested and aiming to learn other languages and technologies
    • Understanding of front-end tech: HTML, CSS, Javascript, D3, React… If you can do it, all the best, but it’s not a strong requirement
    • We use: Ruby on Rails, Ruby, ElasticSearch, Ansible, D3, Javascript (Flight.js, React), Redis, Memcached
    • Good communication skills
    • Enough English knowledge to have meetings with natives

    How we work

    We work for clients and on our products, such as Gobierto, an open government open source platform. We put a lot of thought on what projects we work on and which clients we want to work with. Apart from the output of the work, we value a lot the quality of the process: we like to work fast and efficiently, and that can only be done if everyone is motivated, happy, aligned and believing in what we are doing and who are we working with.

    Our aim as a team is to enjoy the process of building products that we believe in and that put a dent in the universe.

    More info about us in our website: populate.tools

    What you will work on

    You will take part on the development of Gobierto mainly, but also other clients projects and some of our internal tools (we have a few).
